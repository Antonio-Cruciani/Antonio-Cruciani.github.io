About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Gran Sasso Science Institute supervised by Pierluigi Crescenzi and co-supervised by Francesco Pasquale

My research interests are focused on graph theory and algorithms with an emphasis on dynamic networks related topics. The line of my research can be summarized in two main directions: (1) dynamic networks generation models, and (2) algorithms for dynamic networks.
